Binding a Mobile App Record with Mobile Services

To use mobile services, you must represent your mobile app in OpenShift using a mobile app record and that mobile app record must be associated with the mobile service.

The association between a mobile app record and a mobile service uses the OpenShift bind feature.

To associate a mobile app record with a mobile service:

  1. Navigate to the Overview your OpenShift project.

  2. Expand the mobile app record you want to associate by clicking the > icon.

    Your mobile app will be represented by two items in OpenShift, an item in the mobile app records list and an item in the Provisioned Services list. Make sure to expand the item in the mobile app records list.

  3. Note the value for clientId in the Config Info field. You will need this for the next step.

  4. Choose a mobile service from the Integrations list. If an option is not available, you can provision a service by choosing that option and associate the mobile app record later.

  5. Following the Create Binding wizard to associate the mobile app record with the mobile service. You may be prompted for a mobile app record ID. Enter the value you noted from the previous step.
